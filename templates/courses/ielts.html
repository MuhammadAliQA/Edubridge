IELTS Academic va General farqi nima? ▾
Academic — universitet uchun (Reading va Writing qiyinroq). General Training — ish/immigratsiya uchun (Writing osonroq). Ikkalasida Listening va Speaking bir xil.
Qancha vaqt tayyorgarlik kerak? ▾
0 dan boshlasangiz 6–12 oy. Intermediate darajada 3–6 oy. Har haftada 3 ta dars bilan 4–6 oy ichida 6.5–7.0 olish mumkin.
Imtihon qayerda topshiriladi? ▾
Toshkent, Samarqand, Buxoro, Farg'ona va boshqa shaharlarda British Council va IDP markazlarida topshirish mumkin.
Imtihon narxi qancha? ▾
O'zbekistonda IELTS imtihoni taxminan $200–220 atrofida. British Council va IDP orqali ro'yxatdan o'tish mumkin.
